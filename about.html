Our story

Nearly 40 years of doing it right

What started as a small car lot in 1986 has grown into one of Pflugerville's most trusted repair shops — built on the same idea it began with: treat people fairly and fix the car right.

From a car lot to a landmark

How we got here

In 1986, we opened as a small car lot and started doing the occasional repair for neighbors who couldn't find a nearby shop they trusted. Word got around. By 1987 we were a full auto repair shop — and we've been serving Pflugerville, Round Rock and Cedar Park drivers ever since, using the latest tools and equipment while keeping the small-shop honesty people first came to us for.

Today our ASE-certified technicians work on everything from daily commuters to work fleets, foreign and domestic. A lot of our customers have been coming to us for decades — and we know many of them by name.
